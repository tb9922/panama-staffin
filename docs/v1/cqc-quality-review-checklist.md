# V1 External CQC / Quality Review Checklist

Use this with an ex-CQC inspector or quality consultant before declaring V1 inspection-ready.

## Review Inputs

- Portfolio board pack covering a full operational week.
- Portfolio dashboard screenshots for at least three homes.
- Manager Actions export/list with overdue and escalated examples.
- CQC Evidence pack output.
- Agency override report.
- Audit Calendar completion/sign-off examples.
- Outcome Metrics dashboard.

## Reviewer Questions

- Does the board pack show which homes are under control and which are hiding chaos?
- Are red/amber/green bands credible for a care-home operator, not just technically plausible?
- Can an inspector trace a red signal to owner, action, deadline, and evidence?
- Are CQC quality statements and evidence gaps clear enough to drive inspection prep?
- Does the agency guard prove internal cover was tried before agency was used?
- Are overdue actions escalated early enough for safeguarding, clinical, and governance risk?
- Are there any missing evidence categories that would weaken an Outstanding-style narrative?
